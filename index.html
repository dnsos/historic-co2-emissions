Historic CO2 emissions in city centres

An attempt to visualise emission developments and interpret correlations with historic events

A prototype developed as part of the class "Visualisierung von Klima-Szenarien" at the University of Applied Sciences Potsdam and within the SENSES project.

Data

The dataset used is provided by the Potsdam Institute for Climate Impact Research (PIK) and contains CO2 emissions in gigatons of carbon[?] (GtC) from 1750 to 2012 in form of gridded data. Cells for the different city centres were isolated and used in the visualisation.

Visual Encoding

The visualisation consists of two views. One shows the EMISSIONS and uses a colour range from white to black to indicate the amount of emissions. The DEVIATIONS view indicates the annual deviation (in %) from the previous year and this way focusses on rapid increases and decreases in emissions.
